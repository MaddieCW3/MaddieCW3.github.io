Introduction

Party Hops

Party Hops is a microbrewery that has successfully infused their beer with cannabis. They are located in San Luis Obispo county with a storefront in Grover Beach (near their grow operation).

Target audience

The people who would use this site are those who may want to visit and/or order some of the beer from this company. The audience will be twenty-one years of age and older who drinks socially and uses cannabis socially as well.
