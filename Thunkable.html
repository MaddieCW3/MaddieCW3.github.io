Thunkable Android App: Test of the Morons





Write Up

Our app was coded with Thunkable, also known as MIT App Creator.
The purpose of our app is to have the player go through a series of "tests" to determine if they are a moron or not, while generating revenue through displaying advertisements.
The screenshots show all of our screens, which include the levels, the losing screen, the winning screen, and the branding screen.
